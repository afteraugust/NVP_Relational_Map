## The Idea
Formalize a service-learning partnership with State University's Urban Studies department. [[Prof. Sarah Lin]]'s graduate students would work directly with our programs — providing fieldwork labor, data analysis, and research capacity in exchange for academic credit and real-world experience.

## Why It Matters
- **Capacity multiplier:** 5-10 grad students dramatically expand our canvassing and programming bandwidth
- **Research credibility:** Academic partnership strengthens every grant application
- **Data:** Professional-grade analysis of our survey data
- **Publications:** Potential for academic publications citing our work (visibility in funding circles)
- **Pipeline:** Grad students may become long-term allies or employees
