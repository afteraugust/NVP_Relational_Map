## The Idea
Monthly storytelling series in the [[Riverside Gazette]] profiling neighborhood connectors — the people who bridge communities, organize informally, and make neighborhoods work. Tom Nguyen would write the profiles; we'd identify the subjects from our network.

## Why It Matters
- **Marketing:** Regular media presence without paid advertising
- **Network effects:** Tom's previous article brought [[Prof. Sarah Lin]] inbound — imagine monthly
- **Community building:** Profiles celebrate neighborhood connectors, reinforcing the value of our work
- **Content:** Profiles become content for our own communications, grant applications, social media
- **Recruitment:** Profiled individuals become natural candidates for [[Neighborhood Leadership Academy]]
