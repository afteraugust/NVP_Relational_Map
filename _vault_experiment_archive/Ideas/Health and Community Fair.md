## The Idea
Combined health + community fair partnering YMCA youth programs with Riverside Health Clinic health screenings. Could be a standalone event or a special edition of the block party series. Free health screenings as a draw, community activities alongside.

## Why It Matters
- **Engagement driver:** Free health screenings bring people who might not attend a "community event"
- **Health equity:** Addresses real need in underserved neighborhoods
- **Cross-sector partnership:** Health + community development strengthens grant narratives
- **New contacts:** Health-focused attendees expand our network into a different population segment
- **Funder appeal:** Health + community intersection is a strong funding narrative
