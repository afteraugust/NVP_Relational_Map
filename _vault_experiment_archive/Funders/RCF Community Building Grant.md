## Overview
Annual grant program supporting neighborhood-level community building. Awards $25-50K for 12-month projects. Our application centers on the [[Neighborhood Leadership Academy]] with supporting data from [[Know Your Neighbor Survey]].

## Requirements & Eligibility
- 501(c)(3) nonprofit (we qualify)
- Serving Riverside neighborhoods (we qualify)
- Must demonstrate community input in program design (survey data)
- Preference for collaborative approaches (our partnership network)

## Our Angle
Strong application because: survey data demonstrates need and community engagement, multiple partnership letters (YMCA, Grace Church), Diane Okafor's endorsement, and the Leadership Academy model directly aligns with RCF's theory of change.
