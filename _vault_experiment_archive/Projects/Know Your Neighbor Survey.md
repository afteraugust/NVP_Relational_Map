## Overview
Door-to-door and event-based survey mapping relationships, needs, and assets in our target neighborhoods. Produces data that strengthens grant applications and reveals network opportunities. Also serves as a relationship-building tool itself — the act of surveying creates connections.

## Partners & Stakeholders
- Team only (Maria leads, August and David support at events)
- Potential: [[State University Urban Studies]] grad students as canvassers ([[University Service-Learning Partnership]])

## Funding
Internal — no dedicated funding. Canvassing done by team and volunteers. University partnership would significantly expand capacity.
