## Overview
Structured program matching local businesses with community organizations for mutual benefit — businesses get community visibility and customer loyalty, orgs get sponsorship and in-kind support. Formalizes what's already happening informally between our network and local merchants.

## Partners & Stakeholders
- [[Main Street Alliance]] — access to 40 local merchants via [[Carlos Mendez]]
- [[Priya Sharma]] — potential pilot participant (Sharma's Kitchen)

## Funding
Unfunded. Could be self-sustaining if structured as a facilitated matchmaking service with modest membership fees. Could also be included in future grant applications as a sustainability strategy.

## Milestones
- [ ] Develop program framework and value proposition
- [ ] Pilot with 3-5 business-org pairings
- [ ] Evaluate pilot and iterate
- [ ] Scale if successful

## Notes
- This emerged organically from the block party partnerships — the concept is already proven informally
- Would strengthen our funding narrative: "community-driven sustainability"
