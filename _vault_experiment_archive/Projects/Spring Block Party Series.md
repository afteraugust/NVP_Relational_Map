## Overview
Series of three block parties across different neighborhoods in Ward 4 during spring 2026. Primary community engagement and marketing vehicle. Each event combines food, music, youth activities, and a "Know Your Neighbor" station where we collect survey data and build relationships.

## Partners & Stakeholders
- [[Sharma's Kitchen]] — catering at reduced cost (marketing exchange)
- [[Grace Community Church]] — venue for one event (fellowship hall parking lot)
- [[Main Street Alliance]] — merchant sponsors and vendors via [[Carlos Mendez]]
- [[Riverside YMCA]] — youth activity stations via [[Fatima Al-Rashid]]
- [[Rachel Torres]] — Facebook promotion (drove 40+ to last event)
- [[Tom Nguyen]] / [[Riverside Gazette]] — event coverage

## Funding
