**About**
Three funding areas: community organizing, education, and music. On community organizing, they make a sharp distinction between community service (giving to others) and community organizing (projects originated by and from the community itself) — they only fund the latter. Inside Philanthropy

**Fit for NVP:** The community organizing bucket is the most relevant angle. NVP's resident-led model, where neighbors initiate and sustain change themselves, maps well to their philosophy.

**Grant specifics:** Grants range from $1,000–$20,000, with most in the $10,000–$15,000 range. [Triple-funds](https://triple-funds.com/tf-entry/grants-from-sparkplug-foundation/) They run two cycles per year (spring and fall), making 20–25 grants per cycle. They explicitly want proposals that are seed-oriented — demonstrating how a small, one-time grant can have big impact and influence systems for long-term change. [Sparkplugfoundation](https://www.sparkplugfoundation.org/information/)

**Eligibility notes:** Requires 501(c)(3) status or a US-based fiscal sponsor, annual budget under $1M, and activities within one year. [Triple-funds](https://triple-funds.com/tf-entry/grants-from-sparkplug-foundation/) NVP's Raft Foundation fiscal sponsorship satisfies the fiscal sponsor requirement now; independent 501(c)(3) pending.

**How to Engage as a Prospective Grantee**

The process is: (1) create an account in their application portal, (2) complete a basic eligibility questionnaire, (3) submit a Letter of Intent (LOI). LOI deadlines are mid-October (fall) and spring cycle dates TBD. [Sparkplugfoundation](https://www.sparkplugfoundation.org/information/)

They explicitly try not to make demands that get in the way of grantees' work, and offer assistance/feedback along the way. They're aware many groups doing great work are new to grant applications. [Sparkplugfoundation](https://www.sparkplugfoundation.org/) Low-barrier, approachable tone — worth reaching out to Mulligan informally before applying.

**Application tips from their own guidance:** Be highly specific about steps, people, materials, and how you'll know if you've succeeded. Use plain language. Show how this seed funding enables long-term sustainability. [Sparkplugfoundation](https://www.sparkplugfoundation.org/information/)
