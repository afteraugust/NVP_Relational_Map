## About
What this org does and why it matters to our work:
- Claude: They coordinate resilience efforts across Colorado and define resilience as the ability of communities to "rebound, positively adapt to, or thrive amidst changing conditions." They're actively building out a network of resilience hubs and recently partnered with the Center for Climate and Energy Solutions to develop a Resilience Hub Toolkit for the North Front Range [Colorado](https://coresiliency.colorado.gov/news-article/the-colorado-resiliency-offices-2025-resiliency-recap) — which includes Boulder. NVP's steward model is essentially building the _social layer_ that resilience hubs need to actually function. This is a relationship play, not a grant application — you'd want to get on their radar as a partner organization.
- [Colorado Resiliency Office Website](https://coresiliency.colorado.gov/)
