# How to Use Calendar

Calendar adds a small calendar widget to your sidebar that integrates with your Interaction notes.

---

## Opening Calendar

Click the calendar icon in the right sidebar. If it's not visible, Cmd+P → "Calendar: Open view."

---

## What It Shows

The calendar displays a monthly view. Days with interaction notes show a dot. The number/size of dots indicates how many interactions were logged that day.

Click any date to see the notes associated with it.

---

## How It Connects to Your Vault

Calendar looks for notes with dates in their filenames or frontmatter. Since your Interaction notes are named `YYYY-MM-DD Title` and have a `date` field in frontmatter, Calendar picks them up automatically.

This means:
- Scroll through the calendar to see which days had conversations
- Click a date to jump to that day's interactions
- Spot patterns — are there weeks with no interactions logged? That's a gap worth noticing.

---

## Tips

- Calendar is read-only — it doesn't create notes, it just shows when notes exist.
- It's most useful during weekly reviews: scroll back through the past week and confirm that all conversations were logged.
- If a date doesn't show a dot but you know you logged an interaction, check that the note's filename starts with `YYYY-MM-DD` and the `date` field in frontmatter matches.
